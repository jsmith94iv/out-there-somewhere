About

Out There Somewhere emerged from the hazy intersection of dreams and reality, crafting ethereal soundscapes that blur the lines between consciousness and cosmic drift.


Our music weaves shimmering guitars with submerged melodies, creating a sonic journey through nebulous realms of shoegaze and dream pop. Each song is a star in our constellation, guiding listeners through the vast emptiness and beautiful chaos of existence.


Formed in the twilight hours of urban isolation, we channel the whispers of distant galaxies into harmonies that resonate with the souls of stargazers and dreamers alike.
